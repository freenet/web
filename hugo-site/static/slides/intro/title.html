Why Freenet?

  • A handful of corporations — Google, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, Cloudflare — effectively control the internet
  • This contradicts the internet's original design goal and the idea that it "routes around censorship"
  • Root cause: the client-server model, popularized by the World Wide Web in the 1990s
  • Consequence: governments now fight over who gets to censor — not whether censorship should happen
  • Freenet's goal: restore the internet's original promise through decentralization — and today I'll show you we've done it
