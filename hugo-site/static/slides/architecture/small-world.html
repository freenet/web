Small-World Network Topology

  • Each peer has a location (0.0 - 1.0)
  • Connect to nearby peers + some distant peers
  • O(log N) routing to any location
  • Same topology for millions of peers
The small-world topology is key to Freenet's scalability. It's the same principle behind "six degrees of separation" - any two people on Earth are connected by about 6 friend-of-friend links.
