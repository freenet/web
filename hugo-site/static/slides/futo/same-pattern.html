  • This is the slide that ties the whole talk together. Every app on the ecosystem map is the same shape.
  • A UI on top. Under it a Freenet contract that defines the rules. Under that, signed or encrypted state. Under that, the network doing routing, storage, subscriptions, and update propagation.
  • Swap the top two boxes and you get a different app. The bottom half never changes -- that's the substrate everyone shares.
  • A River room and a Delta site are the same kind of object at the network layer. That's the thing we're actually trying to prove.
