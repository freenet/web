SAY (cues):

  • Every Freenet app is the same shape.
  • The top is the app: a UI, contracts that hold the rules, its signed or encrypted state.
  • The bottom is the network everyone shares: routing, storage, subscriptions, updates.
  • Swap the top, you get a different app. The bottom never changes.
  • A contract is roughly a database table with its own rules baked in, and a real app uses several.
