SAY (cues):

  • The slide that ties the whole talk together. Every app is the same shape.
  • Top: a UI. Then a contract with the rules. Then signed or encrypted state. Then the network: routing, storage, subscriptions, updates.
  • Swap the top two boxes, get a different app. The bottom half never changes, that's the shared substrate.
  • A River room and a Delta site are the same object at the network layer. That's what we're proving.
