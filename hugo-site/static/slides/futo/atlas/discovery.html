Atlas: Discovery Without a Gatekeeper

On the web, whoever runs discovery — Google, the app store, the feed — decides what you can find. And they answer to advertisers, not to you.

Atlas is Freenet's discovery layer: a public index anyone can host and verify, with no company between you and what you're looking for.

The aim: rankings you choose, not an algorithm you can't see.

The live Atlas UI, running on Freenet today

  • Discovery is the quiet power center of the web. Whoever runs search, the app store, or the feed decides what billions of people can find -- and they optimize for their advertisers, not for you. That's the gatekeeper.
  • In a network with no platform, there's no company that can own that position. Atlas is our attempt to make discovery a public good: an index anyone can host and verify, where you decide whose rankings you trust.
