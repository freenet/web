SAY (cues):

  • The honest pipeline: a crawler we run fetches pages.
  • An LLM writes a short title, summary, and tags. That becomes a signed entry.
  • The entry goes into an index contract on Freenet. Your browser pulls it and searches locally.
  • We run the crawler and the describing LLM. We decide what gets indexed.
  • Genuinely decentralized already: the index. Any node hosts, serves, and verifies it, it's signed.
  • That split is the whole design, not an accident.
