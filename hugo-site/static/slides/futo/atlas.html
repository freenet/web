Atlas: Discovery Without a Platform

If apps and content don't live on platforms, then discovery can't depend on a platform either.
  • Freenet-native search and indexing
  • A way to find Freenet apps and content
  • Part of the answer to "how does anyone find anything?"

A first version is running on Freenet • github.com/freenet/atlas

  • Discovery is the question everyone asks about decentralized systems: if there's no platform, how do users find anything?
  • Atlas is our first working answer -- Freenet-native search and indexing, running on the network.
  • Be careful with wording: say "a first working version" or "a running version," NOT "we solved decentralized search." It's early.
  • The important thing is that discovery is itself just another Freenet app -- an index that's contract state -- not a central service we operate.
