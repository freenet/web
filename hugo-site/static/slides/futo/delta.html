  • Delta is the proof point that this isn't a chat-only story. It's publishing -- websites and content -- running on Freenet today.
  • You create and update content the way you'd expect. But the content lives as Freenet state, not on a hosting provider.
  • There's no host to pay, and no host that can take the content down. That's the whole point.
