BACKUP (Q&A / accuracy, not for live):

  • "What keeps it alive if no one's reading it?" It stays live while anyone subscribes to it, including the publisher's own peer. Abandoned, zero-demand data does evaporate, by design.
  • Persistence follows demand, not payment. To keep a site up, keep a peer subscribed to it.
